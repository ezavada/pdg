◆ lightOffset()

lightOffset ( Offset offset)

Sets light source with spherical coordinates in radians.

The lightOffset is an Offset with two values representing spherical coordinates in radians: offset.x (ρ/rho): Horizontal angle (azimuth) - rotation around the vertical axis offset.y (θ/theta): Vertical angle (elevation/polar angle) - angle from the horizontal plane

Key Points Offset(0, 0): Light directly in front at horizon level Offset(π/2, 0): Light from the right side at horizon level Offset(0, π/2): Light from directly above Offset(π, 0): Light from behind at horizon level The values are relative to the sphere's center, with the light positioned at a distance proportional to the sphere's radius.
